Show Patience and Empathy

Children may learn and respond differently. Calm, kind reactions help them feel safe and confident to communicate.

WHAT THIS MEANS

Showing patience and empathy means understanding that children with special needs may learn, communicate, or respond differently.

HOW IT WORKS

When parents, teachers, or caregivers respond calmly and kindly, children feel safe and accepted.

WHY THIS APPROACH NEEDED

Children with special needs may face daily challenges such as sensory sensitivity, communication difficulties.
